Player Unknown's Battle Ground (PUBG)

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, commonly known as PUBG, is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game. Developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of Bluehole and later Krafton, the game was first released in March 2017.

Player Unknown's Battle Ground (mobile legents)

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. Released in 2016, it quickly gained a massive player base, especially in Southeast Asia.

The Sims is a popular life simulation video game series developed by Maxis and published by Electronic Arts. It was first introduced in the year 2000 and has since become one of the most iconic and enduring franchises in the simulation genre.

Player Unknown's Battle Ground (dota)

Dota, short for "Defense of the Ancients," is a popular and influential sub-genre of real-time strategy (RTS) video games known as Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA). The original Dota mod was created as a custom map for the game Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos and its expansion, Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne. It was first developed by a community of modders and later refined and expanded upon by various contributors.
